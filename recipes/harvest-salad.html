Harvest Salad

Spinach salad with blue cheese, dried berries, olive oil, if you can't find olive oil substitute it with canola oil or even coconut oil

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup crumbled cheese
  • 1 bunch spinach/kale ,rinsed and torn into pieces
  • 1/3 Olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dried berries
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 table spoons red wine vinegar
  • salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • one avocado sliced(optional)

Harvest Salad

Spinach salad with blue cheese, dried berries, olive oil, if you can't find olive oil substitute it with canola oil or even coconut oil

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup crumbled cheese
  • 1 bunch spinach/kale ,rinsed and torn into pieces
  • 1/3 Olive oil
  • 3 spoonful jam
  • 1/2 cup dried berries
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 table spoons red wine vinegar
  • salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • one avocado sliced(optional)

    • Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F(190 degrees C). Arrange berries in a single layer on a baking sheet. toast in oven for 5 minutes, or until nuts begin to brown.
    2. In a large bowl, toss together the spinach/kales, dried berries, cheese, tomatoes, avocado(optional) ,and red onion.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together jam, vinegar, olive oil, pepper, salt. Pour over the salad just before serving, and toss to coat.

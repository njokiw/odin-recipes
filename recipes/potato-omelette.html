potato omelette

Potato omelette with some chilli,tomatoes, green pepper and butter

Ingredients

  • 4 large peeled potatoes
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 large green/yellow/red pepper
  • 2 spoonsful melted butter
  • 1/2 spoonful chilli
  • salt to taste
  • a bunch of dhania

Steps

  1. Grate your potatoes in a large bowl
  2. Mix with the eggs
  3. Chop your tomatoes, pepper,dhania and mix them with your potato mix
  4. Add the chilli
  5. Add some salt
  6. Fry in low heat using the buttter as oil until it's golden brown on both sides-the best pan to use is non-stick pan
  7. Serve for 4

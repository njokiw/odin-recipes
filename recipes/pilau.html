Pilau

Beef pilau and vegetable salad(optional)

Ingredients

  • 1kg Rice
  • 3/4 chopped beef
  • 4 spoonfuls Tomato paste
  • 4 large red onions
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Tropical spices
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 medium cabbage
  • 4 medium carrots
  • Salt to taste

Pilau Directions

  1. In a medium size sufuria boil the beef to soften. Add a spoonful of both ginger and garlic paste for the meet to tenderrize while the meat is still boling.
  2. After the meat is ready, chop 2 onions, in another sufuria and add vegetable oil fry the onions till golden brown ,add some ginger and garlic paste to the onions and stir them for 30 seconds.
  3. Add tomato paste and stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Then add at least 4 spoonfuls of tropical spices and stir for 30 seconds.
  5. Add the boiled beef, stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. While waiting for the meat to cook, wash the rice.
  7. After,the meat is ready, add 2 litres of water, cover and wait for it to boil,then add the rice.
  8. Cook for 15 minutes while covered, then serve it in a serving bowl and wait for it to cool.
  9. Serve with salads or plain.

Vegetable salad(optional)

  1. Chop the cabbage.
  2. Grate the carrots.
  3. Chop the onions in circles.
  4. Mix them.
  5. Add some vegetable oil in a sufuria and heat for 10 seconds.
  6. Then add the vegetable mix to the oil stir for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve in a salad bowl.
  8. Serve with pilau.

Mashed potatoes and beef stew

Mashed potatoes and beef stew with peas(optional)

Ingredients

  • 7 large peeled potatoes
  • 3 Spoonfuls melted butter or substitute with margarine
  • Black pepper
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1/2 kg chopped beef
  • Vegetable oil
  • Ginger and garlic paste
  • Green pepper
  • A bunch of dhania
  • Salt to taste

Directions for mashed potatoes

  1. Boil the potatoes in a sufuria till they are mashable.
  2. Add butter or margarine when the potatoes are still boiling.
  3. Then sprinkle some black pepper before the potatoes are ready.
  4. Add some salt to taste.
  5. When they are ready, mash them till they are.
  6. serve in a serving bowl an wait to cool.
  7. Serve with beef stew.

Directions for beef stew

  1. Boil the beef meat, add ginger and garlic paste to tenderlize.
  2. Chop green pepper, tomatoes ,onions and carrots.
  3. Add some vegetable oil and onions to the meat when it's soft (drain any water in a bowl that is remaining in the beef, before adding.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. If you opted to go with the peas , add them and make sure they are boiled prior to adding them.
  6. Stir and wait to cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add salt to taste.
  8. Add some water, or the drained soup if you had any from step 3, wait for it to cook till ready.
  9. Once ready serve in a serving bowl wait for it to cool and serve with the mashed potatoes .
